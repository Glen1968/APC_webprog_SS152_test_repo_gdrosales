Asia Pacific College

	Asia Pacific College (APC) was established in 1991 as a non-profit joint venture
	between IBM Philippines and the SM Foundation. It was envisioned as a learning
	institution that produces graduates who fulfill information technology industry demands.

	Asia Pacific College emphasizes industry-academe links, orienting their programs
	and faculty toward industry needs. APC's cornerstones are its IT-embedded curriculum,
	industry-experienced faculty and its six-month internship program.
